How to participate

Register & submit an extended abstract

To take part, register and submit a short extended abstract. Submissions are not competitively reviewed — every abstract from a registered participant is accepted. They simply help us form balanced design groups and tailor the workshop activities to the contexts you bring.

Length
2–3 pages
Format
Springer LNCS — template ↗

Your abstract should describe

  • Context The institutional or course context in which you work, or plan to work, with hackathons.
  • Motivation Your motivation for attending, and the design challenge or question you bring to the workshop.
  • Experience Any prior experience with hackathons or project-based learning (if any).
