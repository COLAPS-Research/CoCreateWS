Workshop goals

What we set out to achieve

Five interconnected goals guiding the workshop design and facilitation.

01

Introduce the framework

Share the CoCreate framework and empirical findings from three-institution pilot implementation with the TEL community.

02

Apply to your context

Enable participants to use the framework's guidelines and decision points for their own institutional setting through guided design activities.

03

Critical reflection

Foster reflection on challenges and opportunities of integrating hackathons into formal curricula, including assessment, equity, and scalability.

04

Collect feedback

Gather structured community feedback to directly inform the final CoCreate framework deliverables.

05

Build a network

Create a cross-institutional network of stakeholders interested in educational hackathon design, contributing to the CoCreate community of practice.

06

Publish outcomes

Produce a technical report reflecting workshop outputs, including anonymised blueprint examples and synthesis of participant insights.
